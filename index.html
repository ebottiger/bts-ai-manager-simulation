The Answer

The Core Conversation Framework

Four questions. In this order. Every time. The sequence is the skill — understanding before advising is what separates a good intention from a conversation that actually moves someone forward.

01
Where are you with AI right now?
Surface their current relationship without judgment. Let them name it to tame it. Resist the urge to respond — just listen for what's real.
02
What's underneath that?
Listen for whether this is about identity, ethics, mastery, capacity — or something else entirely. The answer shapes everything that follows. Stay curious longer than feels comfortable.
03
What would feel like a safe next step?
Shrink experimentation to a manageable, readiness-aligned action. Small and real beats ambitious and avoided. The goal is movement — not a grand plan.
04
Let's define one AI goal for this month.
Translate the conversation into a specific, capability-building behavior with a clear timeframe. One goal. This month. Agreed together.

The sequence matters — understand before you advise.

You'll see this framework come to life in every scenario that follows.
